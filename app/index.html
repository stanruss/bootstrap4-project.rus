Who We Are

At TNT Mate, we are best known for our scheduled LTL service between Canada and the USA. We specialize in the shipment of dry goods. We price by the hundredweight or by the skid and deliver industry-leading transit times to and from the USA… including Alaska.

When it comes to experience, TNT Mate fits the bill. Each member of our executive leadership team brings an average of more than 20 years of hard-earned industry experience to the table. Our team is our main asset as thanks to its consolidated efforts we do our job.
